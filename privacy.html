Privacy Policy for My Mic Pro

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

My Mic Pro ("the App") is a real-time audio utility provided by The My Mic Pro Team. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information, the permissions we require, and our use of third-party advertising and billing services.

1. Core Functionality & Microphone Privacy

Our primary service is to provide a live megaphone experience. We operate under a strict No Data Collection policy for our core features.

  • Microphone (RECORD_AUDIO): This permission is used solely to capture audio for real-time playback to connected speakers.
  • Ephemeral Processing: All audio captured by the microphone is processed exclusively in the device's volatile memory (RAM).
  • No Recording or Storage: Voice data is NEVER recorded, saved to the device's permanent storage, or transmitted to any external servers.

2. Advertising & Third-Party Services

To keep this App free to use, we display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. These third-party services collect data to serve relevant ads and monitor performance.

  • Google AdMob: Collects information such as your IP address, general app usage statistics, and the Android Advertising ID (AD_ID) for personalized advertising and frequency capping.
  • User Consent (GDPR/CCPA): We implement the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request consent from users in regulated regions. You can manage your preferences at any time within the App settings.
  • Google Play Billing: Used to handle voluntary tips and "Pro" upgrades. Transaction data is managed securely by Google; we do not store your credit card or financial details.
  • Policy Links: For more details, please visit Google’s Privacy & Terms.

3. Required Device Permissions

To function correctly, the App requires the following permissions:

  • RECORD_AUDIO: Used to capture microphone input for real-time audio playback.
  • BLUETOOTH_CONNECT: Required to discover and route audio output to connected Bluetooth speakers.
  • BILLING: Required to process voluntary tips and "Pro" status upgrades via Google Play.
  • AD_ID: Used by the AdMob SDK to provide relevant advertising and frequency capping.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE: Required to maintain the audio stream and microphone connection when the app is in the background or the screen is locked.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: Required on Android 13+ to display the active microphone status notification, ensuring the service is not killed by the OS.
  • WAKE_LOCK / INTERNET / ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to prevent CPU sleep during sessions and to load advertisements/consent forms.

4. Data Storage & Safety Summary

  • Local Settings: The App stores your customized settings (e.g., sensitivity, gain) and your "Pro" status locally on your device. This data is not uploaded to our servers.
  • Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Data Encryption: Any data handled by third-party SDKs (AdMob/Billing) is encrypted in transit using secure industry-standard protocols.
  • Data Deletion: We do not store personal user data on external servers. All locally stored settings and cached "Pro" status markers are removed from your device when the App is uninstalled.
  • No Data Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or share user data with any parties other than the advertising and billing services provided by Google.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
