Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

HK Apps ("we," "us," or "our") operates the My Mic Pro mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Audio Data (Microphone)

My Mic Pro requires access to your device's microphone to function as a real-time megaphone. We do not record, store, or transmit your audio data to any external servers. All audio processing happens locally and in real-time on your device.

2. Data Collection & Third-Party Services

We do not collect any personal information directly. However, the app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: Used to show advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers to personalize ads.
  • Google Play Billing: Used to handle voluntary tips. Transaction data is managed securely by Google.

3. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions for core functionality:

  • Record Audio: To capture your voice for the megaphone feature.
  • Bluetooth: To stream audio to your wireless speakers.
  • Foreground Service: To keep the audio active when the screen is locked.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support email provided in the Google Play Store listing.
